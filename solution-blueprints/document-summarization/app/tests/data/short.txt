Modern retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems combine dense vector embeddings with keyword search to improve relevance. Popular embedding models like BGE, Instructor, and NV-Embed excel at semantic understanding while BM25 handles exact matches. Hybrid search scores documents using weighted combinations of cosine similarity and term frequency.
